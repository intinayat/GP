About Route66 cars

Route66 officially launched operations in Bangalore in February 2016 Route66 started with $215,000 in capital and a fleet of seven cars.

Route66 members reserve a vehicle through the website or a mobile app and can use a credit or debit card for payment. Route66 cars also come with GPS-equipped tablets.
